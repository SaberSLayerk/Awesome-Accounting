Owner & Founder

Denise leads with experience, structure, and a client-first mindset.

Denise is the President and Founder of Awesome Accounting, Inc. For more than 25 years, she has helped small to medium-sized businesses with start-ups, planning, funding support, bookkeeping, and budgeting, using Generally Accepted Accounting Practices (GAAP).

In recent years, Denise has expanded her focus into financial transaction strategy—supporting asset funding, business acquisitions (especially manufacturing and capital goods), and working capital planning for growth. Her approach is built on clear communication, strong confidentiality, and helping clients understand the financial structures and programs available to them.

Before Awesome Accounting, Denise was a business owner and comptroller for Accurate Communications in Denver, a telecommunications leader. She also spent 12 years at Procter & Gamble in a manufacturing environment, serving in roles across operations, safety, training, and brand leadership—gaining deep experience in systems, documentation, continuous improvement, and team accountability.

Denise holds a BS degree in Civil Engineering from the University of South Florida and currently lives in Colorado.
